Welcome

Welcome to Smoke Signals: an open-source Tent <-> RSS bridge.

Sign up with your Tent entity and an RSS feed. Smoke Signals will post new RSS items as status posts with links to your account.

Smoke Signals is currently in alpha. Things might change quickly and not work properly. If you need stability you can host it yourself.
